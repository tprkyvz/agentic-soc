
# `docs` – Documentation and Thesis Materials

The `docs` directory is the **documentation hub** of the project. It should
contain everything needed to understand, reproduce, and evaluate your work
without reading the full codebase.

## Subdirectories

- `docs/reports/` – Monthly or milestone-based progress reports.
- `docs/architecture/` – System design, diagrams, and technical descriptions.

You can add more subfolders later (e.g., `user-guide`, `api-reference`)
as the project matures.

## Recommended Practices

- Keep documents in **English**, even if you take notes in another language
  elsewhere. This will simplify sharing and reviewing your work.
- Version your important diagrams and design documents here.
- Use consistent naming, such as:

  - `architecture-overview.md`
  - `data-flow-diagram.md`
  - `evaluation-plan.md`
